about us

This website is built to be used with smart buildings to get time to time information regarding the temperature of their surroundings.

A user can know the temperature of a city or a location even if they are not logged in.

If the users want to know the temperature of the surroundings of their building and for using this website, must register themselves using their building id.

The users can also keep track of any sudden changes in the temperature of their buildings for in such conditions they will be notified with a message on thier homepage.
